## 🏠 Why This Matters

AI is like hiring a house help.

Before they enter your home you verify their National ID, 
their background, their references. You stay alert while 
they work. You check again before they leave.

AI is no different.

It is emerging in every industry — coding, teaching, finance, 
hiring, healthcare. Every day brings a new capability. 
But every new capability comes with a cost.

Just like a house help needs verification and oversight, 
AI needs controls — to prevent bias, data leakage, and misuse.

That is what GRC professionals do for AI.
This repository is a practical toolkit to do exactly that.
